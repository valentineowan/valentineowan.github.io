Teaching

My teaching focuses on assessment literacy, measurement foundations, and practical research skills. I aim for clarity, strong examples, and methods that students can use in projects and field work.

Teaching focus

  • assessment planning and test construction
  • validity, reliability, and fairness in educational decisions
  • basic statistics for research interpretation
  • research design and reporting for student projects
